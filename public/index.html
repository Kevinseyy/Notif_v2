How it works

1. Create or join a group
Create a group and share the invite code with your friends, or enter a code to join an existing group.

2. Signal when you're free
When you're available to hang out, press "Free now". Everyone in your group gets an instant push notification.

3. No spam
The Free now button locks for 10 minutes after you press it, so you won't accidentally send multiple signals.

4. Works everywhere
NOTIF works in your browser and can be installed as an app on your phone or desktop for the best experience. NOTE: You need to enable notification to get notified.
